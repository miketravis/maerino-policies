# Privacy Policy

Maerino ("the app") collects: name and email from your sign-in
provider (Facebook/Google) or directly when you register, and your
chosen color theme.

We use this data to authenticate you and personalize your experience.
We don't sell or share it with third parties. Data is stored on
Google Cloud (US) and is encrypted at rest.

To request access or deletion of your data, email
support@<yourdomain or gmail>, or sign in and choose
"Delete account" in your account settings.
